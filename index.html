IRR Break-Even Gross Revenue Per Day

Shipping investment calculator focused on required daily revenue, year 1 cash yield, and average cash yield over the life of the vessel. Optional back leverage can be turned on or off.

Inputs

Use Back Leverage
Turn leverage on to calculate equity-based cash yield after annual back finance interest expense.
Assumptions: daily gross revenue is flat across the full investment term; annual revenue = daily revenue × 365; dry dock occurs in years 5, 10, 15, etc.; residual value is received in the final year. Back leverage, when turned on, is modeled as interest-only with no amortization.
